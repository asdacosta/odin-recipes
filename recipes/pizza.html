Pizza

Description

Pizza is a popular dish that originated in Italy. It typically consists of a flattened dough base, topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and a variety of toppings such as vegetables, meats, and herbs. It is often baked in a hot oven and served hot. Pizza has become a popular food all over the world and is available in many different styles and variations.

Ingredients

These are the list of ingredients needed for Pizza recipe:

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese
  • Veggies
  • Meat toppings

Steps

    Procedure to make pizza:
  1. Roll out the pizza dough on a slightly floured surface.
  2. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  3. Sprinkle the shredded cheese over the sauce.
  4. Add veggies and desired toppings.
  5. Bake the pizza in a preheated oven for about 15 minutes.
